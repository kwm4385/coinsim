Welcome to CoinSim! These instructions will help you get started with this Bitcoin trading simulator.

Creating Simulations

When you start using CoinSim, the first thing you need to do is create a new simulation. A simulation is a self-contained trading environment in which you hold balances of cash as well as Bitcoin. Trades performed in one simulation do not affect others and you can have up to 10 simulations at a time. To create your first simulation, select the Active Simulation menu and then Manage. On the simulation management page, you can create new simulations and delete existing ones. To begin creating a new simulation, click the New Simulation button.

Each simulation has several parameters you can set. These include:

  • Name - A descriptive name to help you identify the simulation.
  • Start bank - The amount of (fiat) money you will start with, in dollars.
  • Trading Fee - The percent commission deducted for each trade that you make. This can be set between 0-100%. (A realistic fee would be about 0.2-0.5%.)

These settings cannot be changed once a simulation has been started, so choose carefully!

To delete a simulation, click the trash can icon at the end of its row in the table. A dialog will appear and allow you to confirm your choice. Deleting a simulation will remove it from your simulation list and cannot be undone!

Trading Dashboard

Your trading dashboard provides an overview of your balances and trading history for the active simulation. If you have multiple simulations, you can switch between them at any given time from the Active Simulation menu. In the top banner you will find information on the active simulation such as the funds you started with and the transaction fee. Directly below, your current cash and Bitcoin balance is displayed. Your net worth is the sum of the your cash on hand and the value of your Bitcoins at the current market price.

Buying and Selling

At the heart of any good trading strategy is of course buying and selling! CoinSim currently supports one-time, immediate trades using the BitcoinAverage Price Index, a weighted average of the world's largest Bitcoin exchanges. The price is updated and cached by the CoinSim servers every 60 seconds, and trades are always executed with the most current price.

To buy or sell Bitcoin, select the appropriate link in the sidebar and enter the amount you wish to trade. The calculated fee and total will be displayed and you can then confirm the trade. If you have sufficient funds in your active simulation's account, the trade will be executed immediately and added to the trade history on your dashboard.

Charts

To help you plan your trading strategies, CoinSim allows you to view several third-party exchange price charts all in one place. Simply select the Charts link and the charting service you would like to view. To view the chart in a new window, click the link in the upper right corner.
