About

CoinSim is a Bitcoin trading simulator that allows everyone to get to familiarize themselves with the Bitcoin market and practice their trading strategies. Manage your virtual funds by buying and selling Bitcoin in order to maximize your net wealth! Prices are updated in real time to reflect the current market so you can hone your investing skills without putting your assets at risk.

Features include:

  • Multiple simulations with independent balances
  • Customizable starting balance and trading fee
  • Trading dashboard with embedded charts
  • Instant buying and selling
  • Price index powered by BitcoinAverage
  • Mobile friendly design
